About
THE CLUB

Run With Nimma CK is a running community based in Bengaluru, founded by competitive runner and coach Nanu Nimma CK. With 8+ years of experience in competitive running, athlete training, the club is built to help runners train smarter, stay consistent, and achieve their personal best. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where runners of all levels can improve through structured training, scientifically designed workouts, and disciplined coaching.

Whether you are preparing for your first 5K, aiming to improve your 10K or half marathon, or chasing a marathon personal best, our training programs are designed to guide you step by step.

At Run With Nimma CK, we believe running is not just about speed it’s about community, consistency, and long-term progress.

Train together. Improve together. Grow together.
What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01

INCLUSION

No pace requirement. No judgment. Every runner from couch to 5K to ultramarathon is equally welcome here.

02

CONSISTENCY

Showing up matters more than showing off. We celebrate the habit of running, not just the finish line moments.

03

COMMUNITY

Our community is built on passion, support, and the love for running. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned runner, CK Running Club welcomes everyone to train, grow, and celebrate every mile together

04

GROWTH

We believe every runner has another gear. Structured coaching and peer accountability unlock it.

05

JOY

Running should be fun. Post-run chai, terrible running jokes, and sunrise views are non negotiable.

06

SAFETY

Warm-ups, cool-downs, reflective gear, and buddy systems.
