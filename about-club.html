About the Club

OUR STORY

Six years. One city. Thousands of miles run together.

How It Started

BORN ON
THE ROAD

Nanu Nimma CK Run Club started in 2019 as a WhatsApp group of seven friends who kept showing up to the same corner of Cubbon Park at 6am. No structure, no plan — just the shared madness of preferring a run to an alarm snooze.

Within six months, the group had grown to over 80 runners. Within a year, we had pace groups, coaches, trail runs outside the city, and our first race-day contingent at the Bengaluru 10K.

Today, Nanu Nimma CK is home to 842+ runners — from absolute beginners to sub-4-hour marathoners. The city has changed. Our members have changed. But the core reason we lace up hasn't: community first, always.

Milestones

THE JOURNEY

2019

The First Morning

Seven runners. One park. The idea that showing up beats sleeping in.

2020

Growing Through the Pandemic

Solo runs, virtual challenges, and a community that held together when the world shut down.

2021

First Official Pace Groups

Beginner, intermediate, and advanced groups formed. Every runner finally had their tribe.

2022

Hesaraghatta Trail Run — Inaugural Edition

Our first off-road event. 42 runners, one muddy trail, zero regrets.

2023

500 Members & First Marathon Finishers

A major milestone in size — and in personal bests. Eight members ran their first full marathon.

2024

Dedicated Coaching Programme

Structured 12-week plans introduced for 5K, 10K, half and full marathon distances.

2025

842 Members Strong

Three weekly runs, a packed event calendar, and the same spirit from day one.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01

INCLUSION

No pace requirement. No judgment. Every runner — from couch-to-5K to ultramarathon — is equally welcome here.

02

CONSISTENCY

Showing up matters more than showing off. We celebrate the habit of running, not just the finish line moments.

03

COMMUNITY

The roads are more interesting with others. Our strongest bonds were formed at mile 18 of a long run.

04

GROWTH

We believe every runner has another gear. Structured coaching and peer accountability unlock it.

05

JOY

Running should be fun. Post-run chai, terrible running jokes, and sunrise views are non-negotiable.

06

SAFETY

Warm-ups, cool-downs, reflective gear, and buddy systems — we take care of each other on every run.
