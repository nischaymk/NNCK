Why Join Nanu Nimma CK

WHAT YOU GET

Nanu Nimma CK membership isn't just a run three times a week. It's structured coaching, community accountability, event access, and the kind of support that turns good intentions into real habits.

Your first run is free — no forms, no commitment. Just show up at any of our weekly runs. After that, membership is ₹499/month or ₹4,999/year.

3 Weekly Group Runs
Tuesday speed, Thursday easy miles, Sunday long run — all coach-led with warm-up and briefing included.
Personalised Training Plans
12–20 week structured programmes for 5K through ultra. Adapted to your life, schedule, and goals.
Community & Accountability
WhatsApp group, Strava club, and pace partners who show up even when you don't feel like it.
Priority Event Access
First registration slots for all Nanu Nimma CK events, trail runs, and race-day pace groups.
Nutrition & Recovery Support
Monthly workshops on fuelling, sleep, and injury prevention from coach Arjun and guest specialists.
Member Milestones & Merch
Personal bests celebrated publicly. Annual club kit at discounted rates. You earn the orange.

Common Questions

GOT QUESTIONS?

Do I need to be fit to join?

Absolutely not. Our Thursday easy run and Couch-to-5K programme are specifically designed for complete beginners. No pace is too slow.

What does membership cost?

₹499/month or ₹4,999/year. Your first run is always free with no commitment. Annual membership saves you two months.

What time do runs start?

Tuesday: 6:00am · Thursday: 6:30am · Sunday: 5:30am. We start on time. Arrive 5 minutes early to sign in and warm up.

Can I join mid-training cycle?

Yes. Group runs are open year-round. Training plans start at set dates — check the Runs page for upcoming start dates.

Is there a minimum age?

Members must be 16 or older. Runners aged 16–18 require parental consent to join official runs.

How do I reach the coach directly?

Coach Arjun is active daily in the member WhatsApp group. You can also speak to him before and after every group run.
