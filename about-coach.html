How CK Coaches

COACHING PHILOSOPHY

01

ATHLETE-FIRST

No two runners are alike. Every training plan is adapted to the individual — their life schedule, stress load, sleep, injury history, and goals.

02

SCIENCE + FEEL

Zone 2 base, periodisation, progressive overload — the fundamentals are non-negotiable. But listening to your body will always overrule a spreadsheet.

03

PROCESS OVER OUTCOME

Chase the habit, not the PR. When the process is right, the results follow. Arjun trains runners to fall in love with the daily work.

04

EASY RUNS MATTER

80% of your mileage should feel almost too easy. Most runners go too hard, too often. Arjun is relentless about protecting recovery.

05

MENTAL EDGE

Race day is 50% physical, 50% mental. Visualisation, race strategy, and managing the inner critic are trained alongside intervals.

06

COMMUNITY POWER

The best performance enhancer is a training partner who shows up. Arjun builds groups, not just athletes.

Working with Arjun

WHAT TO EXPECT

When you join Nanu Nimma CK, you get direct access to Arjun through the weekly runs and our club WhatsApp group. He observes your form, adjusts your pace zones, and checks in when you've missed a session.

For members on a structured training plan, Arjun provides weekly check-ins, monthly video reviews, and personalised race-day strategy sessions in the two weeks before an event.

He's not the type to yell from the sidelines. He's more likely to be running next to you, telling you to slow down — and explaining exactly why that's the fastest way to get faster.
