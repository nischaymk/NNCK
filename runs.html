Before You Come

WHAT TO BRING

👟

RUNNING SHOES

Any pair you're comfortable in. We'll tell you what's wrong with them — kindly.

💧

WATER BOTTLE

For Sunday runs especially. Hydration stops are communal — everyone looks out for everyone.

📱

STRAVA / WATCH

Track your run and join our Strava club. Every km counts — even the recovery ones.

🌅

AN EARLY ALARM

We start on time. The route won't wait. But we promise the sunrise is worth it.

🧡

OPEN MIND

All paces, all ages, all backgrounds. Leave the ego at home, bring the enthusiasm.

☕

POST-RUN PLANS

We usually head to a chai stall after Sunday runs. Not mandatory. But honestly, why wouldn't you?
